Our Commute Journey

Pioneering Urban Micro-Mobility Care

Founded in 2019, VoltGlide started as a small local garage fixing flat tires for early e-scooter riders. As electric commuting exploded, we engineered professional-grade electronic diagnostics systems to handle controller chips, custom firmware calibrations, and advanced high-capacity lithium battery welding.

Today, we are the leading urban micro-mobility repair laboratory, dedicated to keeping riders moving cleanly, safely, and cost-effectively.

Our Static-Free Electronics Laboratory

Unlike standard bicycle repair shops, VoltGlide runs a clean room chamber dedicated exclusively to micro-electronics repair. We solder under stereo microscopes, use digital oscilloscopes to inspect controller signals, and execute battery cycle-capacity load testing inside safety-certified steel battery chambers.

Your scooter's electronic heart is highly sensitive. We protect it from ESD damage, incorrect voltage spikes, and improper thermal sealings.

Core Values We Solder By

The principles that guide our technical laboratory and customer service standards.

Lithium Safety Absolute

All battery diagnostics and balance welding are completed inside fireproof steel enclosures under static-free conditions.

E-Waste Reduction

We actively salvage circuit board components and correctly recycle lithium cells, minimizing local urban e-waste footprint.

Honest Appraisals

If your scooter controller doesn't need to be replaced, we don't bill you. We repair rather than discard whenever possible.

Transparent Service Guarantees

Every repair includes clear pricing and service promises so you know exactly what is covered and what to expect.
