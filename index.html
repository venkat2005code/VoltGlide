Our Static-Free Electronics Laboratory

Unlike standard bicycle repair shops, VoltGlide runs a clean room chamber dedicated exclusively to micro-electronics repair. We solder under stereo microscopes, use digital oscilloscopes to inspect controller signals, and execute battery cycle-capacity load testing inside safety-certified steel battery chambers.

Your scooter's electronic heart is highly sensitive. We protect it from ESD damage, incorrect voltage spikes, and improper thermal sealings.
